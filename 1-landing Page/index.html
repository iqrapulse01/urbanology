Key Services

Scooter Pick-Up, Recharge, and Redeployment

Efficient Fleet Collection: Our mobile teams, equipped with vans, collect scooters in need of charging or maintenance. Sustainable Charging Solutions: Scooters are recharged using renewable energy sources at centralized charging hubs. Strategic Redeployment: Fully charged scooters are returned to high-demand areas based on real-time data, maximizing availability and user satisfaction.

Comprehensive Maintenance and Repairs

Routine Maintenance: Regular checks for brakes, tires, and motor performance ensure operational safety and longevity. On-Site Repairs: Address minor issues in the field to reduce downtime. Advanced Repair Facilities: For complex issues, scooters are brought to fully equipped repair centers.

Fleet Optimization and Analytics

Demand-Based Redistribution: Real-time insights help strategically deploy scooters where they’re needed most. Performance Monitoring: Detailed analytics and reports to track fleet health and improve operational decisions.

Sustainability and Eco-Friendly Solutions

Battery Recycling: Eco-friendly programs for battery disposal and recycling. Green Energy: Charging hubs powered by renewable energy sources to reduce carbon footprints.
